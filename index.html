About Me

Akif Ejaz

Since childhood, I have had a keen interest in computers and electronics. The day I used computer for the first time, I checked every single option, had to know how everything worked. That interest brought me to the field of Engineering which further fueled my interest. The day I created my first AI Face Detection Project, I was overwhelmed. In University, I won Computer & Engineering competition, by giving the solution and implementation of a real-world problem.

    In this regard, I am looking forward to pursuing my field of interest to next level. I'm confident that having internship with EPFL would provide unparalleled learning opportunities. Also, in a diverse cultural setting, with so many options and opportunities, experience world-class practical life experience will change my life and my future. I believe that this ‘Summer internship EPFL’ is the best mean of exploring knowledge and hands on experience.
